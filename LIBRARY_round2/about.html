Our mission

Our mission at the Library and Information Sciences Department is to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to harness the power of information effectively. We are committed to providing a comprehensive education that prepares students for careers in library sciences, information management, and data analytics. Through a blend of theory and hands-on experience, we aim to nurture professionals who can curate, organize, and disseminate knowledge, contributing to the advancement of education, research, and society.
