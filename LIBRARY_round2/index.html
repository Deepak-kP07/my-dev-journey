"The Library and Information Sciences Department at Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies - Srikakulam Campus is a cornerstone of knowledge management and information access. Our department plays a pivotal role in equipping students with the skills and expertise needed to navigate the information-rich world of the digital age.

We offer a comprehensive curriculum that encompasses the study of library sciences, information management, digital archives, and data analytics. Our experienced faculty members bring a wealth of knowledge and practical experience to the classroom, ensuring that students are well-prepared for the dynamic field of information sciences.

In today's information-driven society, the ability to organize, access, and utilize knowledge is paramount. Whether you aspire to become a librarian, an information manager, or a data analyst, our department provides the knowledge and tools to excel. We are dedicated to fostering a love for learning and a commitment to preserving and disseminating knowledge effectively.

Join us at the Library and Information Sciences Department and embark on a journey of information discovery, management, and contribution to the intellectual growth of our society."
