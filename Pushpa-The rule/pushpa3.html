The Story Continues

Pushpa 2: The Rule picks up right where The Rise left off. After establishing his dominance in the red sandalwood syndicate, Pushpa Raj faces new challenges as he expands his empire beyond the borders of Seshachalam. With enemies gathering at his doorstep and love blooming in his heart, Pushpa must navigate the dangerous waters of power, politics, and betrayal.
