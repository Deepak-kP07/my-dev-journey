Overview

Pushpa 2: The Rule is the much-anticipated sequel to the blockbuster movie Pushpa: The Rise. Directed by Sukumar, the film continues the journey of Pushpa Raj and his adventures in the world of red sandalwood smuggling. With stellar performances and gripping storytelling, this movie is set to break box office records.

About the Movie

The movie explores the rise of Pushpa Raj as he faces new challenges and adversaries. With breathtaking action sequences, emotional depth, and chart-topping music composed by Devi Sri Prasad, Pushpa 2 promises an unforgettable cinematic experience. The film is slated for a grand release worldwide, aiming to captivate audiences across all age groups.
