The Story Continues

Following the events of Pushpa: The Rise, the story follows Pushpa Raj as he expands his empire while facing new challenges from both law enforcement and rival gangs. With Bhanwar Singh Shekhawat seeking revenge and new threats emerging from the underworld, Pushpa must protect his territory while keeping his loved ones safe.

Set against the backdrop of red sandalwood smuggling in the Seshachalam forests, this high-stakes drama promises intense action, powerful dialogues, and emotional depth that made the first part a blockbuster success.
